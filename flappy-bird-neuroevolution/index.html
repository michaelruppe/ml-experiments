What's going on here?

A population of 'birds' tries to navigate the course. The more successful birds are more likely to reproduce. Small mutations affect the behaviour of offspring.
Each 'bird' is contrlled by a neural-network which has some information about the virtual world - like the bird's position and distance-to-wall, for example. The neural-net can only control the bird's flapping.
