What's going on here?

The Doodle Guesser starts out untrained - randomly guessing what you've drawn - pretty sucky. When you train the Doodle Guesser, a neural network is trained with a set of real, labelled doodles of cats, fish and houses and guitars - 800 of each The labels are used to make corrections to any incorrect guesses by tweaking the neurons within the network. The Doodle Guesser can still only guess at what you've drawn, there's no label. Hopefully after a round of training it's not too shabby.
